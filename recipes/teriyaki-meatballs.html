Teriyaki Meatballs

Description

A brilliant midweek slow cooker recipe, throw it in the pot before work, and return to an uncious tasty masterpiece

Using frozen meatballs, and a few other ingredients makes this a really low touch, but ultra tasty dish

Ingredients

  • 24 precooked, frozen meatballs
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/3 cup beef broth
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch

Steps

  1. Place meatballs in a lightly oiled slow cooker
  2. To make teriyaki sauce: in a medium bowl, whisk together remaining ingredients until smooth
  3. Pour sauce over meatballs. Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours, or until sauce bubbles and thickens. To speed up thickening, you may turn the slow cooker to high for the last hour of cooking time
  4. Serve over hot cooked rice
