Air Fryer Chicken Wings

Description

Who doesn't love a whole mess of wings? Especially on game days?

These Air Fryer wings are ready in a snap, and have a perfectly crispy salty skin, with just a hint of garlic and lemon.

Perfect for smothering in your favourite wings sauce, or eating naked...the wings, not you!!

Ingredients

  • 1lb chicken wings, split into flats and drummettes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp lemon pepper

Steps

  1. Thoroughly pat dry chicken wings with paper towels and place in a mixing bowl.
  2. Coat in the oil and then sprikle on the garlic salt and lemon pepper. Toss to evenly coat with seasoning
  3. Place on an air frying basket, spacing evenly and air fry at 400F for about 8 minutes per side, or a total of 16 mins, or until chicken wings are crisp and golden brown on the outside.
